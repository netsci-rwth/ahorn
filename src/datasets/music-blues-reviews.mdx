Hypergraph where nodes are Amazon reviewers and hyperedges are reviewers who reviewed a certain product category (different types of blues music) within a month timeframe.
The dataset was derived from the product reviews collected by Jianmo Ni, Jiacheng Li, and Julian McAuley.

<Alert type="warning">The original dataset is temporal, which this version does not capture, yet.</Alert>

## Attributes

- `genre : string`: The genre of blues music that the reviewers co-reviewed.

For simplicial complexes, the suggested construction to satisfy simplicial closure is to interpret the category label as a set.
Non-maximal simplices get the category of all maximal simplices they are contained in.
