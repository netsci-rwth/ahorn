## Overview

The CiteSeer co-citation network is a hypergraph where nodes are scientific papers from the CiteSeer dataset and hyperedges are sets of papers that are co-cited together by another paper.
Each node is labeled with the paper's research field, chosen from six computer science topics: Agents, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Databases (DB), Information Retrieval (IR), Machine Learning (ML), and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).
This dataset is commonly used as a benchmark in hypergraph learning tasks.
