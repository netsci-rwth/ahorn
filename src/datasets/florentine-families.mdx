## Overview

The Florentine families network represents marriage ties among prominent families in Renaissance Florence.
We lifted the graph to its clique complex using [`toponetx.graph_to_clique_complex`](https://pyt-team.github.io/toponetx/api/transform.html#toponetx.transform.graph_to_simplicial_complex.graph_to_clique_complex), so cliques become higher-dimensional simplices.

This dataset is suited to test graph-based methods on a small historical social network and to exercise AHORN's clique-complex dataset structure.
