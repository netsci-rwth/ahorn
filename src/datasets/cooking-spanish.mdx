## Overview

This dataset is the `Cooking` subset restricted to recipes labelled as `spanish`.
It keeps the ingredient nodes that participate in at least one recipe of this cuisine.
Raw data was obtained from the [“What's Cooking?” Kaggle competition](https://www.kaggle.com/c/whats-cooking).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Two recipes with only one distinct ingredient have been dropped from this dataset.

## Attributes

- `ingredient : string`: The name of the ingredient corresponding to a node.
