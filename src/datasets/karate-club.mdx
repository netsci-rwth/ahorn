We lifted the graph to its clique complex using [`toponetx.graph_to_clique_complex`](https://pyt-team.github.io/toponetx/api/transform.html#toponetx.transform.graph_to_simplicial_complex.graph_to_clique_complex), so cliques become higher-dimensional simplices (edges, triangles, and higher cliques).
