## Overview

This temporal higher-order network represents email communication at a European research institution.
Nodes are email addresses, and each hyperedge contains the sender together with all recipients of an email sharing the same timestamp.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Singleton hyperedges, such as emails sent only to oneself, are omitted in this dataset.

## Attributes

- `time : datetime`: The email timestamp in ISO 8601 format.
