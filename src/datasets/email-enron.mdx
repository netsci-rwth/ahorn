## Overview

This temporal higher-order network represents email communication in the Enron corpus.
Nodes are email addresses, and each hyperedge contains the sender together with all recipients of an email message.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Singleton hyperedges, such as emails sent only to oneself, are omitted in this dataset.

## Attributes

- `email : string`: The email address corresponding to a node.
- `time : datetime`: The email timestamp in ISO 8601 format.
