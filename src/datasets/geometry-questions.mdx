Hypergraph where nodes are users on [MathOverflow](https://mathoverflow.net/) and hyperedges are sets of users who answered a certain question category (derived from different tags involving geometry).
The dataset was derived from the [Stack Exchange data dump](https://archive.org/details/stackexchange).

## Attributes

- `category : string`: The category of the question that the users co-answered, derived from the question tags.

For simplicial complexes, the suggested construction to satisfy simplicial closure is to interpret the category label as a set.
Non-maximal simplices get the category of all maximal simplices they are contained in.
